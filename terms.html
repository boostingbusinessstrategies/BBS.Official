Commitment to Our Customers

At BBS, we are dedicated to providing tailored database solutions for the E-commerce, healthcare, and other key industries. Our focus is on delivering high-quality, customized solutions that meet your specific business needs, ensuring satisfaction and data protection.

Security and Privacy Policy

We take data security very seriously. BBS strictly adheres to GDPR and CCPA regulations, ensuring all customer data is handled with confidentiality. We only collect essential information (email, name, surname) to facilitate communication and project execution. With customer consent, we may retain this data for future updates or promotions; otherwise, it is securely deleted upon project completion.

Refund and Return Policy

We offer a 7-day satisfaction guarantee for all database projects. During this period, clients can request up to three free modifications to ensure the delivered solution meets their expectations. If, after these adjustments, and within 7 days, you are not satisfied, we will refund 100% of the paid amount. No refunds or returns will be processed after the 7-day period. Any change requests after this period will incur additional costs. Clients acknowledge and accept these terms at the time of purchase.

Service Guarantee

All BBS services include a 7-day quality guarantee, offering three free modifications during this time to ensure customer satisfaction. After the 7 days, any further modifications will be charged according to the current rates.

Shipping Policies

The delivery of services will be conducted via email. However, customers have the right to request delivery through Messenger or WhatsApp for their convenience. The delivery will be completed within a maximum of 5 business days. If you do not receive any response from us within 48 hours, it may be due to high demand. You can contact us to inquire about the status of your order and see if we can expedite the process. Please note that the database will be sent in a compressed ZIP format; however, customers can request it in an uncompressed format according to their needs. The database will not be sent encrypted; this must be done by the owner or responsible engineer to facilitate its rapid shipping and installation. If you have not received your order and wish to request a refund, you can do so through our communication channels; however, a recovery penalty of 15% of the paid amount will be applied, which is acknowledged and accepted by the customer upon purchase.

Database Content Policies

1. Prohibited Information

Requests for columns containing sensitive information such as emails, phone numbers, passport IDs, or other personal sensitive information are not allowed, in compliance with CCPA and GDPR regulations. We only request names and surnames as maximum information.

2. Optimization

All databases include indexes to ensure optimal performance and are delivered ready for immediate use. No temporary tables or CTEs will be created; this must be handled by the owner or engineer post-purchase. Modifications involving these elements will void the database warranty.

3. Modifications

Up to three free modifications can be requested within a 7-day guarantee period. Additional modifications or requests outside this period will incur extra charges.

4. Delivery and Guarantee

Customized databases will be delivered within a maximum of 5 business days. If you are not satisfied with the result, we offer a money-back guarantee.

5. Payment

Payment must be made before the development process begins. Payments are handled through PayPal, and pricing varies according to the selected plan. If the full payment for the selected plan is not received, either due to accident or error, we will contact you to confirm whether you prefer a refund for the amount paid or wish to complete the remaining payment to proceed with the project. If you wish for a refund due to an incomplete payment, the 15% penalty will not apply, as it was an erroneous charge. Additionally, in the case of payments made in excess of the cost, the remaining amount will be refunded, and if the payment is less than the cost, we will contact you as mentioned earlier.

Database Extension Policies by Plan

These changes come with a 5-day guarantee, allowing up to 2 additional adjustments or edits for free within this period.

1. Basic Plan

Customers on the Basic Plan may request up to 4 complete tables or 4 additional columns per order. If more tables or columns are needed, a new request can be made within 24 hours after the requested changes are made.

2. Standard Plan

The Standard Plan allows for up to 4 complete tables or a total of 20 additional columns per order. As with the Basic Plan, a new request can be made within 24 hours for additional changes.

3. Business Plan

The Business Plan offers unlimited changes, ensuring all customer needs are met.

Policies for Existing Database Modifications

1. Guarantee

Modifications to existing databases come with a 7-day guarantee, allowing clients to request additional changes free of charge during this period.

2. Recommendation

We recommend maintaining a backup of your database before requesting any changes. BBS is not responsible for alterations to the database structure due to requested modifications.

3. Accepted Formats

We only accept modifications for MySQL and SQLite databases. Files must be submitted in their original export format, compressed into a ZIP file. We do not require direct access to your server to make these changes.

Upcoming Services

In the near future, BBS will offer additional services, including support for NoSQL, AWS databases, advanced query execution, data analysis solutions, and more. For the moment, we only work with MySQL and SQLite until future updates in the coming months.
