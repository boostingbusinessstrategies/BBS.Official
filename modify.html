Modify Your Existing Database

Enhance Your Database Today!

Before proceeding, please ensure you have backed up your existing database. Modifying your database without careful consideration may lead to unforeseen issues. We only accept MySQL and SQLite databases, and they must be provided in their original export format, compressed into a ZIP file. Please refer to our Terms and Conditions for more details.

Payment Information:

The cost for modifications is based on the complexity and size of your database. Our fees are as follows:

  • $149 for databases with 5 to 8 tables (up to 5,000 records)
  • $849 for databases with 8 to 12 tables (up to 25,000 records)
  • $1,999 for databases exceeding 12 tables (more than 60,000 records)
Please note that a 10% penalty applies for any refunds, and modifications are subject to our guarantee of 7 days for changes. For more details, consult our Terms and Conditions.
