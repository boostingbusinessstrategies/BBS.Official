Frequently Asked Questions

What types of databases do you offer?

We specialize in providing custom databases primarily built with MySQL, which ensures robust performance and scalability. Our databases seamlessly integrate with platforms like AWS and Oracle to offer you comprehensive and flexible solutions tailored to your specific needs. Whether you need a simple setup for a small business or a complex system for a large corporation, we have you covered.

How long does development take?

Our standard development time is typically 2 business days. However, the actual time frame may vary depending on the complexity of your project. We prioritize delivering high-quality solutions within an efficient turnaround time to keep your business running smoothly. Our goal is to ensure you experience minimal downtime and maximum productivity.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept payments via PayPal and credit/debit cards through PayPal. Transactions are processed securely through the PayPal account of Aaron Obando, our founder, ensuring a safe and reliable payment experience. This gives you peace of mind knowing that your payment information is handled securely.

Can I request changes after delivery?

Absolutely! We offer up to 3 free revisions within a 7-day period to ensure your complete satisfaction with our services. Depending on your service plan, you may be entitled to additional modifications, ranging from 2 to 10, and a guarantee period extending from 3 to 10 days. Any changes beyond this period or exceeding the number of included revisions may incur additional fees. Our goal is to make sure you are fully satisfied with the final product.

Do you support editing existing MySQL databases?

Yes, we can edit existing MySQL databases, even those not originally developed by us. The cost for modification depends on the complexity and data volume of the database. Service fees range from $119 to $1,999. For more detailed information, please refer to our terms and conditions. This allows us to tailor your existing database to better meet your evolving business needs.

Is my data secure?

Your data security is our top priority. We strictly comply with GDPR and CCPA regulations to ensure your data is handled with the highest level of confidentiality and protection. Our comprehensive security measures safeguard against unauthorized access and data breaches, giving you peace of mind.

What are your support options?

We offer continuous, high-quality support to ensure your databases remain operational and efficient. Our dedicated support team is available Monday to Friday, from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM (Pacific Time) and 12:00 PM to 8:00 PM (Eastern Time), as well as on Saturdays from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM (Pacific Time) and 1:00 PM to 5:00 PM (Eastern Time). We are here to help you every step of the way.

Do you provide assistance with modifications?

Yes, we provide support for modifications to your databases, although we do not offer data entry or query services. Our goal is to ensure your database continues to meet your business requirements. We strive to offer you the flexibility and support needed to adapt to your evolving needs.

Can you develop SQLite databases?

Yes, we can develop SQLite databases. Although SQLite offers less flexibility compared to MySQL, it can still be an excellent choice for smaller projects or applications with more limited database needs. We ensure that even our SQLite solutions are tailored to meet your specific business requirements.

Do you have a satisfaction guarantee?

Yes, we are committed to your satisfaction. We offer a satisfaction guarantee on all our database projects, including up to 3 free revisions within a 7-day period, to ensure you are completely satisfied with the final product. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction is unwavering.

What happens if I need further modifications after the guarantee period?

If you require additional modifications after the guarantee period, we are happy to assist for a reasonable fee. Our goal is to provide ongoing support and ensure that your database continues to serve your business needs effectively. We are dedicated to your long-term success.

What is the guarantee period for extensions?

The guarantee period for extensions varies by plan. For Basic and Standard plans, you receive 3 days of guarantee with up to 2 modifications per order. In the Business plan, you have unlimited changes during the 3-day guarantee period. Modifications of databases from other providers or developers have a 7-day guarantee with up to 10 modifications if they are complex and have more than 5,000 records. For databases with 0 to 4,000 records, the guarantee period is 7 days with up to 5 modifications.
